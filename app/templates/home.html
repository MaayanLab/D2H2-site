Diabetes Data and Hypothesis Hub

Diabetes Data and Hypothesis Hub (D2H2) is a platform that will facilitate data-driven hypothesis generation for the diabetes and related metabolic disorder research community.

From 1980 to 2014 the number of people with diabetes grew from 108 million people to 422 million people. Additionally, it directly cause the death of 1.5 million people, almost half of which were younger than 70 years old (WHO 2021). This resource aims to advance our knowledge surrounding diabetes and enable discovery of novel therapeutics. Some other resources that can be utilized to enable diabetes discovery are avalible on the resources page.
