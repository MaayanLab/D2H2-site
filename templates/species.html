An Interactive Gene Expression Viewer

Boxplot Express enables interactive visualization of gene expression data from Microarray and RNA-seq experiments from the Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO). The user can pick a study from the table below to compare the expression of single genes across experimental groups. The expression of the gene will be displayed as a boxplot, with the conditions on the x-axis and normalized expression levels on the y-axis.

Boxplot Express was developed by the Ma'ayan Laboratory, and it is funded by NIH grants R01DK131525 and RC2DK131995.
